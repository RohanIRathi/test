About Us



The Information Technocrat's Student Forum is a club formed by the students of the IT department of VIIT. WE MAKE IT HAPPEN.

Codechef Campus Chapter is the subsidary of ITSF which organises Coding Contests on Codechef and webinars on Competative Programming.

ITSF (Information Technocrats Student Forum) is a cell of the IT department, VIIT Pune, which was started in the year 2005. Since then it has been working to provide a platform for all-round development of the students. Every year the committee conducts various workshops and session to keep the students acquainted with the latest technologies. The workshops are conducted according to the changing trends in the industry. ITSF not only focuses on the development of technical skills of the students, but also the development of the students as whole. Apart from the technical activities, ITSF also conducts and support various extra-curricular, social, environmental and cultural activities. The students of the committee actively take part in technical, social as well as the cultural activities.
