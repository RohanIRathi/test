Greetings from ITSF VIIT!

This is a simple hero unit, a simple jumbotron-style component for calling extra attention to featured content or information.

ITSF (Information Technocrats Student Forum) is a cell of the IT department, VIIT Pune, which was started in the year 2005. Since then it has been working to provide a platform for all-round development of the students.

Every year the committee conducts various workshops and session to keep the students acquainted with the latest technologies.

Apart from the technical activities, ITSF also conducts and support various extra-curricular, social, environmental and cultural activities.

Codechef Campus Chapter

Codechef campus chapter is a part of ITSF. Started in 2019, the goal of Codechef campus chapter is to develop the programming skills of students, their logical abilities, and to prepare them for competitive coding
